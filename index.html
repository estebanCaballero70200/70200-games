In a realm where ancient magic protects the last surviving bastions, dark forces are breaking through the borders between worlds. As the guardian of the Arcane Bastion, you must restore its power, awaken its defenders, and hold the line against each invading wave.

The Río de la Plata prairie is stirring: Anahí has been kidnapped. Malevo must return to his old outlaw ways and begin a journey of redemption, guided by San La Muerte, to stop the Luz Mala.

Matrero is a 2D narrative exploration and platforming adventure where the myths and legends of the Río de la Plata come alive. With scenarios and characters inspired by the gaucho imagery of Blanes, you will choose paths and perfect your skills to unveil a beautiful story of love and destiny, while discovering an unexplored imaginary: the myths and legends of the Río de la Plata.
